    ABOUT "Kaewhom"

    Kaewhom is made from shattered glass. We changed its appearance
    and use it for essential oil absorbing which can repel bugs or mosquitos.
    Moreover, It can be used for aroma therapy that makes your home smells good
    and makes you relax. Also, its packaging can be used for decoration as well.

    History

    Thailand is located on tropical zone. With a lot of water sources and hot temperature ,
    It is proper to be bugs and mosquitos resident. So bugs and mosquitos have become a problem in every house.
    In Thailand, there are a lot of shattered glass left from industry but to get rid of it is going to be pollution.

    So we brought those problem to solve and then we create “ Kaewhom “ which have light weight , durable , and well absorbing.

    "ECO-FRIENDLY"
